Welcome to The NSW Science Hub

Your comprehensive, interactive learning resource for NSW Science students from Years 7 through 12. Whether you're just beginning your science journey in Stage 4 or preparing for your HSC in Stage 6, we've got you covered.

What You'll Find Here

Comprehensive Lessons

Detailed lesson content aligned with the NESA syllabus, covering all required topics and concepts.

Interactive Learning

Engage with practice questions, worked examples, and interactive activities to reinforce your understanding.

Exam Preparation

Practice questions and revision materials to help you prepare for tests and examinations.

Real-World Applications

Discover how scientific concepts apply to everyday life and current global challenges.

Course Structure

Junior Science (Years 7-10)

Foundation science education covering:

  • Biology: Living organisms, cells, ecosystems, genetics, and evolution
  • Chemistry: Matter, chemical reactions, atoms, and the periodic table
  • Physics: Forces, motion, energy, electricity, and waves
  • Earth & Space: Earth's systems, climate, astronomy, and geology

Senior Science (Years 11-12)

Specialized HSC science courses, each with 8 comprehensive modules:

  • Biology: From cellular biology to ecosystems and disease
  • Chemistry: From atomic structure to chemical synthesis
  • Physics: From kinematics to quantum mechanics

How to Use This Resource

  1. Select Your Year Level - Use the sidebar to choose your year (7-12)
  2. Choose Your Subject - For Years 7-10, you'll see all science content. For Years 11-12, select Biology, Chemistry, or Physics
  3. Browse Modules - Each subject is divided into modules covering different topics
  4. Work Through Lessons - Each module contains detailed lessons with explanations, examples, and practice questions
