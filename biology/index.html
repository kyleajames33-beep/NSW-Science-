Year 11 - Preliminary Course

Foundation modules covering cells, organisation, diversity, and ecosystems

Module 1: Cells as the Basis of Life

Focus: This module explores cellular structure, biochemistry, and the fundamental processes that occur in living cells.

Key Inquiry Questions:

Module 2: Organisation of Living Things

Focus: This module examines how cells are organised into tissues, organs, and organ systems in multicellular organisms.
